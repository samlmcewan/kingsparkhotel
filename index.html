Welcome to the Kings Park Hotel

Situated just 15 minutes from Glasgow’s vibrant city centre, Kings Park Hotel is the perfect destination for both business and leisure travellers. A brief stroll to Hampden Park makes it an ideal choice for sporting and music events.

Our hotel spans two ﬂoors, with all 26 rooms being en suite and conveniently located on the ﬁrst ﬂoor. Each room is equipped with WiFi and a Smart TV for your entertainment

Indulge in a delightful dining experience at restaurant which offers a diverse menu that caters to everyone. Enjoy your meal with relaxing views looking onto Overtoun Park.

The hotel offers four unique function spaces, featuring a main suite that is perfect for weddings and can accommodate up to 180 guests. Each venue is adaptable and can be customised to suit individual events, whether it’s a monthly work meeting or an all-out celebration!
